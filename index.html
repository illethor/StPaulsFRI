Community Garden

Over the past 20 years, we have been providing the community with garden plots, facilities, and equipment needed to grow safe, organic produce for their families.

In addition to growing their own food, our gardeners and the children from our summer day camp, participate in growing food which is then harvested and directly distributed to participants of St. Paul's Family Resources' food bank or to be used in collective kitchens.
