About Us

The Family Resources Institute (FRI) is a community outreach program initiated by members of St. Paul's United Church in 1985. FRI offers a number of ongoing programs, aimed at helping build healthier communities for our children, families, friends and neighbours.

Community Ministry Director

Kristen Hollery

Our Community Ministry Director Kristen Hollery has been with the Family Resources Institute (FRI) since 2009. Before she started this position, she had a long affiliation with St. Paul's.

She was part of the congregation on Dentith Road, where she was baptized and confirmed. At St. Paul's United Church, she coordinates the work of the Family Resources Institute, a community outreach program initiated by St. Paul's United Church in 1985.

She is also one of the co-coordinators of the Sunday School, serves on the Church Promotion Committee, as well as the Worship Committee.
